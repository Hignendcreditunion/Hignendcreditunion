Humble Beginnings

We started as a community initiative in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring fair, transparent banking to underserved members. What began as a small local effort has grown into a modern credit union that combines traditional trust with cutting-edge technology.

Our founders believed that banking should be a partnership, not just a transaction. That philosophy remains at the core of everything we do today.
