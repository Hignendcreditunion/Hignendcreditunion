Contact Support

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password"
  2. Enter your email address or username
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if you don't see it)
  4. Click the link and create a new password

If you're still having trouble, contact our support team.

You can transfer money between your accounts in several ways:

  • Online Banking: Log in and navigate to the "Transfers" section
  • Mobile App: Tap "Transfer" and follow the prompts
  • Phone: Call our automated system at +1-802-572-4519

Transfers between your own accounts are typically instant. There are no fees for internal transfers.

If you notice an unauthorized or incorrect transaction:

  1. Contact us immediately at +1-802-572-4519 or email support@highendcu.com
  2. Provide details about the transaction (date, amount, merchant)
  3. We'll place a temporary credit on your account while we investigate
  4. The investigation typically takes 10-45 business days

For fastest resolution, contact us within 60 days of the statement showing the error.

To deposit a check using our mobile app:

  1. Open the High End CU mobile app
  2. Tap "Deposit" from the main menu
  3. Select the account where you want to deposit the check
  4. Enter the check amount
  5. Take a clear photo of the front of the endorsed check
  6. Take a clear photo of the back of the check
  7. Review and submit your deposit

Most mobile deposits are available within 1 business day. Endorse the check with "For Mobile Deposit Only" and your signature.

If your debit or credit card is lost or stolen:

  1. Immediately: Log into your online banking or mobile app and report your card as lost/stolen
  2. Or call: Our 24/7 support line at +1-802-572-4519
  3. We'll immediately block the card to prevent unauthorized use
  4. A replacement card will be sent to you within 3-5 business days
  5. Review your recent transactions for any unauthorized activity

You're not liable for unauthorized transactions if reported promptly.

Opening a new account is easy:

  • Online: Visit our registration page to open an account in minutes
  • Mobile App: Download our app and follow the new account setup
  • Phone: Call us at +1-802-572-4519 and we'll guide you through the process
  • In Person: Visit any of our 68 branches nationwide

You'll need your Social Security Number, a valid government-issued ID, and funding information to open an account.

Yes, our mobile app employs multiple security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint or face ID)
  • Multi-factor authentication for sensitive actions
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity
  • Real-time fraud monitoring
  • Ability to instantly lock your card from the app

We recommend keeping your app updated and using a secure passcode on your device.

We're committed to transparent pricing with no hidden fees:

  • Checking Accounts: No monthly maintenance fees
  • Savings Accounts: No monthly fees with $100 minimum balance
  • ATM Fees: Free at our network ATMs, $2 reimbursement for out-of-network ATMs
  • Overdraft: $25 fee per occurrence (can be avoided with overdraft protection)
  • Wire Transfers: $15 domestic, $35 international
  • Paper Statements: $2 per month (free electronic statements)

See our complete fee schedule in your account agreement or contact us for details.
