How We Protect Your Money

Bank-Grade Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using 256-bit SSL encryption, the same standard used by financial institutions worldwide.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Optional 2FA via SMS, authenticator apps, or biometric verification adds an extra layer of security to your sign-in process.

Real-Time Fraud Monitoring

Advanced AI systems monitor transactions 24/7 for suspicious activity and alert you immediately of any potential fraud.

Biometric Access

Use fingerprint or facial recognition for quick, secure access to our mobile app without compromising security.

FDIC Insurance

All deposit accounts are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor, providing an additional layer of protection for your funds.

Transaction Verification

We verify unusual transactions through multiple channels and provide detailed transaction histories for your review.

Security Best Practices

Protecting your account is a shared responsibility. Follow these guidelines to keep your information secure.

Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Create passwords with at least 12 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Never reuse passwords across different sites.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA in your account settings. We recommend using an authenticator app for the highest security.

Be Wary of Phishing Attempts

We will never ask for your password via email or text. Verify suspicious messages by contacting support directly at support@highendcu.com.

Use Secure Networks

Avoid accessing your financial accounts on public Wi-Fi. Use a VPN if you must access your account from an untrusted network.

Monitor Account Activity

Regularly review your transaction history and set up account alerts for large transactions, low balances, or suspicious activity.

Keep Software Updated

Ensure your operating system, browser, and antivirus software are always up to date with the latest security patches.

Suspect Fraudulent Activity?

If you notice unauthorized transactions or suspect your account has been compromised, take immediate action.
